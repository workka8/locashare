Privacy Policy

Total Privacy — We Never Access Your Data

Your data is created on your device and is under your control. We do not upload your data to our servers at all.

We Do Not Collect Any Data At All

Loca Share collects absolutely no data from you. We do not track your usage, we do not collect analytics, we do not store any information about you or your device. The app works entirely offline and locally on your device.

Zero data collection means:

  • No usage tracking or analytics
  • No personal information stored
  • No location data sent to our servers
  • No device information collected

Syncing and Backup

You can use Loca Share entirely on your device without signing in. You can back up your data using Apple’s iCloud Backup. This is managed entirely by Apple and follows Apple’s privacy policies.

How Sharing Works

When you enter an address or location in Loca Share and tap Share, iOS opens the system Share Sheet. You then choose the destination app (for example, WhatsApp, Messages, Mail, or others).

  • The content you choose to share is copied to the app you selected, under your direction.
  • Loca Share does not transmit or store this content on our servers. The sending happens inside the chosen app and is governed by that app’s privacy policy.
  • You decide what to share and with whom. You can review the message before sending in the third‑party app.

Updates

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Your rights will not be reduced without your clear consent. Any changes will be posted on this page. Please check regularly to stay informed.
